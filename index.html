Why we built Cove

Texting isn't the same as being there

We built Cove because we noticed something unsettling: you can text a friend every single day and still not have seen them in months. Life gets busy. Work, adulting, the endless scroll of obligations. Before you know it, a friendship you genuinely care about has quietly drifted.

The hard part isn't caring. It's noticing. Group chats stay active, likes get exchanged, but actual time together stops happening. Real connection is built in person, and it takes intention to protect that.

Cove exists to give that intention a home. We track who you've actually spent time with, gently nudge you when someone's been left out too long, and make planning the next hangout as easy as possible. Adulting should never have to cost you your friendships.

Frequently asked questions

Is Cove free?

Yes. Cove is free to start. Join the waitlist now and you'll be among the first to access it when we launch.

Who can see my group?

Only the people you invite. Cove is completely private. No public profiles, no algorithmic feed, no strangers. It's just your people.

How does friendship tracking work?

Cove tracks hangout history within your groups and quietly monitors when someone hasn't been included in a while. If a friendship is wilting, we'll nudge you (not guilt you) so you can act before the drift becomes permanent.

Does everyone in my group need the app?

For the full experience, yes. But you can invite friends directly from the app and it takes under a minute to set up. The more of your crew is on Cove, the better it works.

When does Cove launch?

We're in development now. Join the waitlist and you'll get early access before the public launch, plus updates as we build.

Is it available on iOS and Android?

Cove will launch on both iOS and Android. Sign up and we'll notify you the moment it's available on your platform.
