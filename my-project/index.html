Everything your group needs, finally together

Cove replaces the spreadsheets, group chats, and expense apps your friend group currently cobbles together.

Friend Groups

Create a private space for your crew. See everyone's events, balances, and memories in one shared view — no more hunting across apps.

Trip Planning Hub

Plan group trips without the 47-message thread. Shared itineraries, destination polls, RSVP tracking, and packing checklists — all in one place.

Shared Expenses

Log costs, split evenly or by custom amounts, and track who owes what. No awkward conversations — just a fair, running group balance.

Stay in Sync

Cove notices when someone hasn't been included lately and gently nudges you. Birthdays, streaks, and social health — so no friendship drifts by accident.

Memory Archive

Your group's private story — event albums, shared captions, and "On This Day" moments that resurface the best times and remind you why it matters.
